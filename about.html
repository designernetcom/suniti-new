about us

Crafting Excellence in Fire Protection

We are the fastest Metal Doors Manufacturer based out of Pune. With a factory space of 45000 sqft fully equipped with latest CNC machines and inhouse powder coating facility, we can deliver 50 doors in a single shift which provides superfast service for all customers.

Serving the varied requirements of industries, we take immense pride to introduce ourselves as one of the trustworthy manufacturers and suppliers of Fire Resistant Doors, Fireproof Access Panel, Metal Fire Staircase, Fireproof Glass etc. These doors are known for their features such as light weight, fire resistant surface and negligible maintenance. Our offered range of resistant doors is extensively used in hi-rise buildings, hotels and office complexes. Fire Resistant Doors is available in various dimensions & sizes at economical prices in the market.

Infrastructure

  • Owned premise of 45000 sqft with generator backup for uninterrupted production.
  • 2 sets of CNC Fibre laser for world class cutting.
  • 2 sets of CNC Pressbrake for perfect finishings.
  • 8 MIG welding lines
  • 22 feet oven for powder coating 40 doors in a single batch to avoid shade difference.
  • Dedicated inhouse transport fleet for safe and fast delivery.
  • Highly dedicated installation team with 100% cordless tools for safe installation.

Quality Policy

Every door is tested for sizes, punching, bending, finishing and powder coating before dispatch.

Raw material procured are from the best suppliers in the market eg. Sheet from Arcellor Mittal, Hardware from Assa Abloy, Dorma, Geze, Honeycomb from Honicel, Rockwool from Rockwool India and powder from Vipraj, Nerolac and Joton.

All our doors come with self adhesive protection film and are transported using foam cushioned vehicles.
